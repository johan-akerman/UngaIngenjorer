Mechanical Engineer

Volta Greentech, Stockholm

We are in a crucial stage with our product design and we’re now looking for an ambitious mechanical engineer to join the Volta Greentech team. At Volta, we believe that with the right individuals, we can change the future. We want to develop a flexible production system for seaweed and in order to achieve this, we need engineering expertise. The company is still in the start-up phase and therefore you have to strive in that work environment. A successful candidate will have extensive theoretical and hands-on experience with photobioreactors and/or similar systems.

Your responsibilities include but are not limited to:
- In cooperation with the biotech team develop a production system for macro algae.
- Making sure the system is safe, economical and effective.
- Setting up and installing test-facility with lab equipment.

Desirable skills and qualifications:
- Knowledgeable and interested in land-based algae cultivation.
- Relevant education and work experience.
- Purpose-driven and motivated by making a difference.

Does this sound like you? Please apply below.
